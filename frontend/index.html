Glossary
OPR
Offensive Power Rating — weighted sparse least-squares estimate of each team's share of their alliance's score.
DPR (Supp)
Defensive Power Rating (Suppression Edition) — points suppressed per match (Expected - Actual).
Synergy
Average point boost / enablement generated by the team for their partners when an alliance positively overperforms relative to their OPR.
AOPR
Adjusted OPR — re-solved after crediting back points suppressed by opposing defenders. Measures true offensive capability.
Δ Delta
AOPR − OPR. Large positive delta = this team's offense was meaningfully suppressed by defense.
Residual
Expected alliance score minus actual. Positive = underperformed (candidate for defensive refund).
Refund
Score credited back because the model attributes underperformance to opposing defense. Capped at the positive residual.
Defender
DPR > 1.5× OPR with ≥6 matches and above the significance floor (10% of season max DPR).
Variability
Standard deviation of per-match residuals. Higher = more unpredictable match-to-match performance.
OPR Breaker
Alliance underperformed by >2σ — likely mechanical failure or major penalty. Downweighted to 10% automatically.
Weighting
w = time decay (21-day half-life) × stage (quals 1.0 → finals 1.5) × event size (√(teams/40), clamped).
